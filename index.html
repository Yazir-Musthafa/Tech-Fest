ABOUT CARMTIF

CARMEL Technical and Innovation Fest (CARMTIF) is not just an event; it's a grand celebration of technological prowess and inventive spirit. Nestled within the vibrant walls of Carmel Polytechnic College Alappuzha, CARMTIF beckons students from every engineering discipline to unveil their groundbreaking ideas and projects. This dynamic platform converges creativity, technology, and sustainability, fostering an environment where brilliant minds converge to shape the future of engineering excellence. Join us in this extraordinary journey where innovation knows no bounds, and together, we inspire the realms of technological brilliance.
